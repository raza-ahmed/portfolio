Mobile-first Tour Booking Website

The Challenge

Designing a new Tour and Activities booking feature for mobile Homepage. We wanted our users to not just buy a Forex card from us but use the same card to spend money abroad. Leveraging the existing foreign traveler traffic on our website. Our Google Analytics revealed that around 85% of our customers were smartphone users. So we wanted to give mobile utmost importance, we decided we will go for a mobile-first website.

Initial Research

We were using API provided by Viator (a Tripadvisor company). So, my initial research was reading the API docs to understand the flow of the product. Meanwhile, I collaborated with developers and managers in understanding the overall flow. Once I had an initial idea of the product, I started using the other tour & activities booking websites both national and international. I also booked test orders to understand the flow.

Wireframes

Based on my research and the API constraints, I created a wireframe using Balsamiq. It really helped in our initial discussion and also turned out very useful for developers. Since there was a time constraint the initial wireframes helped developers work on the backend of the project.

Visual Design

After working on several versions of wireframes, I created the visual design.
